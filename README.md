Program Details

The MotorPH Payroll System is a console-based Java application that processes employee payroll using data from CSV files.

The system allows two types of users: Employee and Payroll Staff.

Employees can log in and view their personal information such as employee number, name, and birthday.

Payroll staff can compute payroll for a single employee or all employees for a selected month.

The system reads employee information and attendance records from CSV files. It calculates the total hours worked based on the official working hours of 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, applies a 10-minute grace period for late logins, and deducts a 1-hour lunch break from daily working hours.

After calculating total hours worked, the program computes the gross salary using the employee's hourly rate. Mandatory government deductions such as SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax are then applied to determine the employee’s net salary.

The system displays payroll results for two cut-off periods:

1st Cut-off: 1st to 15th of the month

2nd Cut-off: 16th to the last day of the month

A monthly payroll summary showing total hours worked, gross salary, deductions, and net salary is also generated.
